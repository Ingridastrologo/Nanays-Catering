About Us

Vision

To be the premier catering service provider nationwide, delivering excellence and innovation in every event.

Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality catering solutions tailored to each client's unique event needs, with a commitment to convenience and satisfaction.

History

Founded in 1996 to showcase Nanay Agapita “Pitang” Baranda’s cooking, Nanay’s Catering has grown through client satisfaction and has served major clients like DLSU Medical Center, Red Cross, Casino Filipino, PNPA, Bangko Sentral, and more.
